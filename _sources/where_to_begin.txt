Getting Started
===============

iOS Development
```````````````

A big player in iOS development tutorials, the `Ray Wenderlich website <http://www.raywenderlich.com/tutorials>`_ is an ideal starting point for new developers.

Objective-C
-----------

A good starting tutorial is `How To Unleash Your Inner App Developer
<http://www.iphonelife.com/blog/31369/unleashing-app-developer-within>`_ series.

Swift
-----

Thinkster has compiled a bunch of resources and organized into a semi-tutorial
called `A Better Way to Learn Swift <https://thinkster.io/ios-tutorial/a-better-way-to-learn-swift/>`_.

Android Development
```````````````````

A great tutorial site for all things android is `vogella <http://www.vogella.com/tutorials/android.html>`_.
