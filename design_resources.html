Design Resources¶

Tutorial Sites¶

  • HackDesign: A weekly lesson plan created by design pros and meant for anyone interested in designing for tech. Each lesson is emailed.
  • Design and Code: Designer Meng To wrote a series of books and ebooks, some of which teach design for iOS and design software (Sketch). The approach might require some basic understanding of design or design tools. You may be able to find the book elsewhere online.

Resources¶

  • teehan+lax: iOS sketch and photoshop GUI templates.
  • GraphicBurger: Photoshop templates and mockups.

Inspirational Pages¶

  • Designer News: Good way to stay involved in the design community and hear about what’s happening in the industry.
  • Dribbble or Behance: Designers and creative individuals upload their work. Good places for inspiration.
  • Pttrns: Amazing source of inspiration that shows UI screenshots from apps in the App Store. Includes categories based on UI features and functions.
  • Mobile Patterns: Features screenshots from well designed mobile apps.
